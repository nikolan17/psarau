Frequently Asked Questions

What is the DOGE Stimulus Program?

It’s a private, patriotic initiative created by President Trump in January 2025. It supports hardworking Americans by redirecting savings from reduced government expenses directly to citizens. This is not a government check program.

U.S. citizens age 18 and older with a valid ID can enroll, including Americans living abroad.

Choose a tier — Standard, Premium, or Exclusive — provide your contact and payout details, and complete verification. With pre-order, some citizens may receive funds in 2–3 months, ahead of the nationwide rollout in 2027.

  • Standard: Low contribution, payouts up to about twenty thousand.

  • Premium: Medium contribution, payouts up to about seventy-five thousand.

  • Exclusive: Higher contribution, payouts up to about two hundred fifty thousand.

    • We operate transparently. Enrollment is confirmed in writing, and funds go directly to your bank after verification. Only the official DOGE Stimulus website guarantees your payout.
